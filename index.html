Powered by Intelligence

Built using advanced MediaPipe technology for robust face mesh detection.

Precision Tracking

Maps 468 facial landmarks to translate subtle head movements into smooth cursor control across your entire screen.

Blink to Click

Intuitive interaction. Blink your left eye to left-click, right eye to right-click. Customizable sensitivity thresholds.

Zero Installation

Fully portable executable. No drivers or complex setup required. Just download, extract, and launch.

Setup in Seconds

1

Download & Extract

Download the ZIP file and extract it to a folder of your choice. No admin rights needed.

2

Launch & Allow Camera

Run EyeMouseControl.exe. Your system will ask for camera permission—this is processed locally on your device for tracking.

3

Calibrate & Control

Look at the center of your screen and press 'C' to calibrate. Move your head gently to move the mouse. Blink to click.
